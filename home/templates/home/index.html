Who We Are

Western Communication is a professional 24/7 call center based in Bangladesh, supporting two of the UK’s trusted taxi companies — Western Cars and GM Taxis.

We handle everything remotely: taking bookings via phone, managing customer inquiries, and dispatching rides using the iCabbi dispatch software. Our trained agents provide smooth, accurate, and real-time support that meets UK industry standards.

With dedicated teamwork and deep understanding of UK customer service, we ensure every passenger receives a fast and reliable experience — all from our Bangladesh office.
